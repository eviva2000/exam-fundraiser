About this campaign

Hi!
We are a mixed age high school band from Tallinn and we would like to expand and improve our after-class activity. Unfortunately, there is a limited number of instruments and teaching hours available for students and it is hard to receive financial support to tour. Our financial goal is 50000DKK, for this sum of money we could buy some much needed equipment and hire a tour bus.

That’s were you, our angel, could come in. We are accepting financial, material and professional support. Help us learn and enjoy music!
